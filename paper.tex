\begin{abstract}
A study of the production of \fzero at mid-rapidity in proton-Lead and proton-proton collisions at the centre-of-mass energy $\sqrt{s_{\mathrm{NN}}}=$ 5.02 TeV is conducted with the ALICE detector. The paper illustrates signal extraction of \fzero from contributions of other resonances as well as uncorrelated backgrounds, transverse momentum distribution of \fzero. This paper measures nuclear modification factor at p--Pb collisions at $\sqrt{s_{\mathrm{NN}}}=$ 5.02 TeV to discuss internal structure of \fzero.
\end{abstract}
